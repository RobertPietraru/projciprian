Item Catalogue

Welcome,

What is this site?

This site is meant to help people with different hobbies organise their items, more like a database of items.

How do I use it?

Simply input a username, a password and click register to create your account for free!
After the register, you just need to input your username, password and click login!

To add an item, simply input the Item Name, its description , a photo (optional) and wheter it's on shelf, used or defective and click add item!

Can't I just use a paper to keep track of what I have?

Yes, you can. This app is designed to help the user quickly find items in their collection. It is very helpful if you have a large collection and can't remember what you have, helping you avoid mistakenly buying something you already have.

What if I don't have a computer?

Don't worry! This site is optimized for mobile devices, so you can enjoy your hobby on the go.
